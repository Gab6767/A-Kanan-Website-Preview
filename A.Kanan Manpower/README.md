# A. Kanan Manpower Corporation - Website

A professional recruitment agency website for A. Kanan Manpower Corporation, a POEA-licensed recruitment agency serving overseas Filipino workers.

## Project Structure

```
├── index.html          # Home page - Overview and featured opportunities
├── about.html          # About Us - Company history, mission, vision, values
├── jobs.html           # Job Opportunities - International destinations and categories
├── apply.html          # Application Process - Qualification check and application form
├── process.html        # Recruitment Process - Step-by-step process and selection criteria
├── partners.html       # Employers & Partners - Partner networks and credentials
├── contact.html        # Contact & Branches - Office locations and contact information
├── styles.css          # Global stylesheet - All styling and responsive design
└── README.md           # This file
```

## Pages Overview

### 1. **Home Page (index.html)**
- Hero section with company overview
- Featured job opportunities (Japan, USA, Germany, Middle East)
- Quick job categories
- Latest news & announcements
- Office locations preview
- Call-to-action buttons

### 2. **About Us (about.html)**
- Company history and evolution
- Mission & Vision statements
- Core values (Integrity, Excellency, Leadership)
- Awards and recognitions
- Success stories and statistics
- Why choose us checklist

### 3. **Job Opportunities (jobs.html)**
- Career guidance introduction
- International destinations details
- Job categories with descriptions
- Call-to-action for applications

### 4. **Apply Now (apply.html)**
- Qualification self-assessment
- Requirements (age, experience, language, health)
- Application form with fields for:
  - Personal information
  - Professional information
  - Language skills
  - Preferred branch selection
- Deployment preparation information

### 5. **Recruitment Process (process.html)**
- 6-step recruitment process timeline
- Selection criteria
- Worker safety commitment
- Call-to-action to apply

### 6. **Employers & Partners (partners.html)**
- Why partner with A. Kanan
- Deployment network (7 countries)
- International partners information
- Agency credentials and memberships
- POEA license and PASEI membership

### 7. **Contact & Branches (contact.html)**
- 6 office locations with full details:
  - Davao City (Main)
  - Manila
  - General Santos
  - Cagayan De Oro
  - Zamboanga
  - Nueva Vizcaya
- General contact information
- Contact form
- Licensing verification

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-friendly layout that works on all devices
- **Professional Styling**: Modern color scheme with blue, red, and gold accents
- **Navigation**: Sticky navbar for easy access to all pages
- **Forms**: Application and contact forms with proper styling

## Getting Started

1. Save all HTML files and the CSS file in the same directory
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser
3. Click on navigation links to browse different pages
4. Fill out forms to test functionality (note: forms need backend processing)

## Customization Notes
