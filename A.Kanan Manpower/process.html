Our Recruitment Process

From Application to Deployment Success

Process Overview

Our systematic recruitment process ensures we select only the best-trained and high-skilled Filipino workers for international placements.

1

Application & Initial Review

Submit your application through our branch office or online portal. We review your qualifications and conduct an initial assessment to ensure you meet basic requirements.

2

Interview & Assessment

Participate in comprehensive interviews and skill assessments. Our team evaluates your technical competencies, communication skills, and suitability for the position.

3

Applicant Screening

We carefully select only the best-trained and high-skilled candidates. Screening includes background verification, skills testing, and reference checks.

4

Medical & Wellness Check

Undergo comprehensive medical examinations to ensure you are physically and mentally fit for international work. All required vaccinations and certifications are completed here.

5

Training & Preparation

Receive intensive training and preparation programs. Language training, technical skill development, and pre-deployment orientation are provided.

6

Deployment & Support

Get deployed to your destination country. We provide ongoing support, document assistance, and ensure a smooth transition to your new workplace.

Selection Criteria

We look for candidates who embody these essential qualities:

👨‍💼 Obedient

Respectful of authority and willing to follow workplace guidelines and instructions.

💪 Hardworking

Dedicated and committed to delivering quality work and exceeding expectations.

✨ Honest

Trustworthy and truthful in all dealings, both professionally and personally.

🤝 Polite

Courteous and respectful in communication with colleagues and supervisors.

Worker Safety & Commitment

🛡️ Your Safety is Our Priority

We are committed to providing a safe environment and ensuring assurance for worker safety abroad. Our commitment includes:

  • Thorough verification of employer credentials and workplace safety standards
  • Comprehensive orientation on worker rights and legal protections in destination countries
  • 24/7 support hotline and emergency assistance during deployment
  • Regular monitoring and follow-ups to ensure worker well-being
  • Advocacy and legal support if workers face any issues or disputes
  • Insurance coverage and benefits protection
  • Partnerships with reputable international employers and agencies
  • Compliance with POEA guidelines and international labor standards
