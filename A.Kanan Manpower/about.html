About Our Agency

Over 20 Years of Excellence in Manpower Services

Our History

A. Kanan Manpower Corporation was established in 2003 as Badilla Corporation, and has since evolved into one of the Philippines' premier POEA-licensed recruitment agencies. With over 19 years of dedicated service, we have successfully deployed hundreds of Filipino teachers, nurses, engineers, and skilled workers to opportunities around the world.

Vision

To provide growth opportunities and secure bright futures for Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs) through ethical and professional manpower services.

Mission

To assist clients with total manpower solutions while helping Filipino workers reach their international career goals and aspirations.
