Application Process

Start Your Journey to an International Career

Qualification Check

Before applying, please ensure you meet our basic requirements. Self-assess your qualifications:

📅 Age Requirement

You must comply with Philippine government regulations concerning worker age and international destination requirements. Generally, workers should be at least 21 years old with no upper age limit (varies by destination).

💼 Work Experience

You should have relevant work experience in your field. Good work history demonstrates your skills and commitment. Minimum experience requirements vary by position and destination country.

🗣️ Language Proficiency

English proficiency is required for most positions. Other language skills (Japanese, German, Arabic, etc.) are beneficial and may increase your employment opportunities.

🏥 Health & Medical Fitness

You must be physically and mentally fit to work. Medical clearance is required. You will undergo a comprehensive health examination as part of the application process.

Deployment Preparation

Once selected, we provide comprehensive preparation to ensure you are well-trained before stepping onto foreign land.

📋 Pre-Deployment Orientation

Understand your destination country's culture, laws, workplace expectations, and safety protocols. We conduct thorough briefings to prepare you mentally and practically.

🎓 Technical Training

Receive specialized training for your position, including hands-on practice, certification preparation, and industry-specific skills development.

💬 Language Training

Intensive language courses to improve your communication skills in English and the language of your destination country.

🏥 Health & Medical Preparation

Complete medical examinations and vaccinations as required by your destination country. We ensure all health requirements are met.

📄 Documentation Support

We guide you through all required paperwork, visa applications, and international documentation to ensure a smooth departure.
